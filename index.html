About Us

DTH INTRACON is a proudly local business offering top-quality printing services and sports gear. With over a decade of experience, we have helped individuals and institutions bring their brands and teams to life.

Shaggar Sport Shop, located at Malka Nonno or Ashewa Meda in Shaggar City, is a one-stop shop for all sports materials, uniforms, and custom kits. Our partnership ensures a full solution for clubs, schools, and businesses looking for printing and sporting needs.

What We Do

  • Professional printing (banners, ID cards, event badges, flyers, books)
  • Custom heat press & sublimation printing
  • Design and branding services
  • Custom sportswear: jerseys, tracksuits, socks, and more
  • Supplying sports materials: footballs, cones, whistles, bibs, goals
  • Bulk delivery and school/club supply contracts

Printing Services

We offer modern digital printing with reliable machines and trained staff. Whether you need 1 or 1000 prints, our services are affordable and timely. We support everything from business branding to personal merchandise.

Sport Materials

Shaggar Sport Shop provides durable and quality equipment used by schools, teams, and academies. You can find:

  • Training and match balls (standard and premium)
  • Coaching materials (cones, markers, flags, whistles)
  • Goal posts (mini and full-size)
  • Nets, pumps, and team equipment bags

Sport Wears

We produce custom kits for football, basketball, and volleyball teams. Our fabric is breathable, strong, and easy to clean. We also print names, numbers, and sponsor logos.

Our catalog includes:

  • Football jerseys and shorts (custom design)
  • Tracksuits, polos, socks, training bibs
  • School PE uniforms and graduation sashes
